Company Overview

Katski Flores

Filmmaker · Producer

Seasoned creative executive, writer-director, and producer behind some of Philippine television's most iconic series. Former Creative Head for major studios, specializing in story architecture, brand-aligned development, and content strategy. A pioneer in social media marketing for local cinema, she led the campaigns for Kimmydora and Zombadings: Patayin sa Shokot si Remington.

Michiko Yamamoto

Writer · Producer

A multi-awarded screenwriter in the Philippines and abroad, and one of the most respected and sought-after writers in Philippine cinema. A master of character-driven narratives, her core strength lies in building story and narrative integrity. She is highly production fluent, ensuring that ideas are both powerful and operationally sound. She understands the full lifecycle of content: from development to on-ground production to audience impact.
